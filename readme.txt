Tensai RSS is a Wordpress plugin that allows you show different information in your feeds and your posts. And protect the images in your feeds.

In Spanish: Tensai RSS te permite mostrar diferente informacion en tus feeds y en tus posts.

== Description ==

This plugin allows you choose which parts of your posts will be visible in your post or your feeds. 
Using simple tags in your posts you can show information visible only in your blog or in your feeds. 
This is very helpfull to hide some pointless information to your feed readers and viceversa.
Automatically add an icon to your links to highlight them when your feed content is copied.
You can also replace images for links to your post. No more hotlinking!

Compatible with all Wordpress versions (since 2.1 to 2.8.3)

**In Spanish:**

Este plugin te permite escoger que partes de tus posts seran visibles en tus posts o tus feeds.
Usando etiquetas en tus posts, puedes mostrar informacion solo en tus posts o solo en tus feeds.

Agrega automaticamente un icono a tus enlaces para resaltarlos cuando el contenido de tus feeds es copiado.
Tambien puedes reemplazar images por enlaces a tu post. No mas hotlinking!

Compatible con todas las versiones de WordPress (2.1 hasta la actual 2.8.3)
